## 🌍 GLOBAL COMPETITORS ANALYSIS

### **1. FRESHA (UK - Global Leader)**

**Company Profile**:
```
Founded: 2015
HQ: London, UK
Funding: $317.5M
Revenue 2024: $43.4M (up from $22.3M in 2023)
Growth: 94.6% YoY
Valuation: $1B+ (estimated)
Customers: 190K businesses globally
```

**Business Model**:
```
PRICING:
├── Base Product: FREE
├── Per User: $9.95/month
├── Marketplace Commission: 20% (new customers only)
└── Payment Processing: Small % per transaction

REVENUE STREAMS:
├── Subscription fees: ~40%
├── Marketplace commission: ~40%
├── Payment processing: ~15%
└── Other (ads, premium features): ~5%
```

**Strengths**:
- ✅ Freemium model → massive adoption
- ✅ Marketplace network effect
- ✅ Strong brand recognition
- ✅ Well-funded ($317.5M)

**Weaknesses for Indonesia**:
- ❌ Pricing too high untuk Indo SMEs ($9.95/user = Rp 158K/user)
- ❌ Global focus → tidak localized untuk Indonesia
- ❌ English-first UI → barrier untuk capster lokal
- ❌ Payment integration → tidak support Indonesian e-wallets

**Balik.Lagi Advantage**:
- ✅ Pricing 50% cheaper (Rp 199K-499K vs Fresha $50+/month)
- ✅ Bahasa Indonesia first
- ✅ Local payment methods (GoPay, OVO, DANA)
- ✅ WhatsApp-first notifications (critical di Indonesia!)

**Company Profile**:
```
Founded: 2014
HQ: San Francisco, USA / Poland
Revenue: $50M-$100M (estimated)
Customers: 200K+ businesses
Focus: Beauty & Barbershop
```

**Business Model**:

- ✅ User-friendly interface

- ✅ Booking marketplace
- ✅ Marketing tools included

**Weaknesses for Indonesia**:
- ❌ Expensive untuk Indonesia market (Rp 477K-1.75M/month)
- ❌ Per-user pricing → mahal untuk multi-capster shops
- ❌ Transaction fees → additional cost burden
- ❌ Not localized for Indonesia

**Balik.Lagi Advantage**:
- ✅ Flat pricing (Rp 199K-999K regardless of staff count)
- ✅ No transaction fees (at least Year 1-2)
- ✅ Built for Indonesian barbershops dari awal
- ✅ Capster-to-Capster positioning (insider credibility)
